Olive Spread

This is a really simple dish to prepare, and its always a big hit at parties.My father recommends:

"Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temp. before serving."

"

"In winter, try servimg it warm".

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 cups kalamata olives
  • 1 tsp. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend until smooth as possible.
  2. Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times untl they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with some goat cheese and fresh basil garnish. (optional)
